7 Sheep, 1 Cat, and 1 Dog!

Currently I have 7 sheep, 1 cat named Pippy (I hate that name), and 1 dog named Brody. We get our sheep seasonally; in the spring, fall, and summer they graze in our pasture, and in the winter they live in a barn in New Hampshire. Brody is a rescue dog from Alabama. He was found on the streets at eight weeks old and has always had a crooked tail. Our cat is a mess and very overweight. She mostly just hides all day.
